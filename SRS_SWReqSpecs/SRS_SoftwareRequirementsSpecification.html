1Introduction

Uvents is a mobile application that brings the community together by allowing personal users to create events (which can be set as a group event or a date event), commercial users to list their promotions (which can be seen by the personal users and added to their events), and admin users to manage event policies.

1.1Purpose

The purpose of Uvents is to provide personal users a two-way experience by allowing them choose to participate in events created by other users and publish their own events. Additionally, users will have access to a list of promotions offered by local businesses and can add those promotions to their events. Uvents will receive revenue by having commercial users pay to have their promotions listed in the application. Finally, Uvents provides a convenient way for people to easily arrange local events that bring revenue to the local economy and provide recreation for the local residents.

1.2Scope

Uvents is an application for people to post activities/dates and for people to find and join those activities/dates. Businesses can also pay for a select spot on the application for consumer users to view their activities.

The application will allow consumer users to:

  • View activities posted
  • Join an event
  • Chat with those in the activity to establish details
  • Post a group event
  • Post a one-on-one date with the opposite sex
  • Post a group date for 4-6 couples
  • Join a date
  • The consumer user that posted the event/date would have control of said event

The application will allow commercial users to:

  • Post activities that feature their businesses
  • Pay for select spots where their activity will appear

Uvents will help people meet new friends while experiencing an event together. Uvents will also allow those consumer users to post a date or sign up for a date. Businesses will have the ability to promote their events for a price. This feature will create revenue.

1.3Product Overview

Uvents is a social app that will provide opportunities to meet new people while doing fun activities. Events will be able to be created by businesses and individuals in order to facilitate these opportunities. Businesses can create events which can be joined. Funding for the app will come in the form of businesses bidding for priority listings for their sponsored activities. The application will provide a simple experience so that focus can be placed on the activities and social connections of participants themselves.

1.3.1Product Perspective

The application is like Mutual and Facebook in that it allows for the formation of social connections and event planning.
